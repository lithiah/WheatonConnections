Wheaton Connections

This website allows you to browse the pre-approved connections available at Wheaton College (MA). Connections are sorted by number of courses within the connection (always either two or three), the divisions of the courses, and the major and minor departments of the courses. In addition, one specific course can be selected, and the connections that include that course will be displayed. (Only courses that are part of a connection are listed.)

Credit where credit is due.

This website was created by Sara Zhang and Lithia Helmreich of Wheaton College for a computer science course homework assignment (#11 to be exact). All connection information was taken from the Wheaton College website connections listings, found here.
